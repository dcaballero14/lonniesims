Legislative Highlights (referenced in sources)

The summary below reflects publicly documented legislative activity during Lonnie Sims’s service in Oklahoma House District 68 (2018–2024), based on official records and publicly available sources. Descriptions are presented neutrally and do not assert specific bill numbers or outcomes unless explicitly documented by cited sources.

  1. Backing Public Safety & Law Enforcement
    Representative Sims consistently supported legislation that bolstered law enforcement resources and officer safety. This included backing measures for enhanced training, recruitment incentives, and equipment support for local sheriffs and police departments.
  2. Border Security & Immigration Policy
    During the 2023 legislative session, Sims joined a bipartisan group of lawmakers in a fact-finding trip to the U.S.–Mexico border to study enforcement challenges and supported resolutions emphasizing stronger border security.
  3. Property Protection & Fraud Prevention
    Sims voted in favor of bills aimed at tightening identity verification standards and enhancing penalties for property fraud.
  4. Fiscal Responsibility & Government Efficiency
    Supported balanced-budget priorities and measures promoting transparency and accountability in state government.
  5. Rural Infrastructure & Emergency Preparedness
    Advocated for infrastructure funding benefiting rural communities and supported legislation strengthening first responder preparedness.
